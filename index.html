Instructions

Build towers using your mouse and the menu, by clicking where you want them to go.
To cancel building, click when the outline is red.




Upgrade towers to increase their damage and speed.
To view a tower's sub-menu, click on it.


The monsters will evolve depending on what you build.
There is a small chance of more powerful monsters spawning:




Red bugs have no special mutations.
Yellow lightning bugs have extra speed.
Blue fortified bugs have extra health.
